Haji Mohamed Ibrahim Igal

The man who erected the scafold for Somaliland

Here is a timeline of Mohamed Igal's life and acheivements.

  • 1928 - Igal was born in Odweyne.
  • 1960 - He served as the first Somali's defence minister.
  • 1962 - Also the first education minister.
  • 1967 - Igal became the prime minister of Somalia.
  • 1969 - Abdirashid Ali Sharmarke and Igal were elected as the president and prime ministers of Somalia respectively.
  • 1976 - Igal was imprisoned by Barre.
  • 1985 - Igal was released from prison and cleared of charges.
  • 1992 - Igal was came to the office of Somaliland.
  • 2002 - Igal died in South Africa.
